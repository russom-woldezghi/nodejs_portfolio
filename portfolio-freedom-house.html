Freedom House

Freedom House is an NGO that promotes the spread of freedom and democracy around the world through research, advocacy, and programs that support human rights activists. Freedom House well-known analytical reports include: Freedom in the World and Freedom of the Press.

Freedom House's original website was written on ColdFusion and hosted on an IIS server. With the growing popularity of Freedom House's publications, the website needed to splay a visually compleming site. Legacy data generated over 20 years needed to be restructred and presented with interactive maps. With the help of developer firm Forum One, I worked to migrate legacy data from multiple sources and a produce visually interactive features using the Drupal 7 platform.

Interactive custom maps were developed for Freedom House reports and regional pages. Maps provided visitors with interactive experience that users to compare data of neighboring countries.

Project Details:

  • Custom map development
  • Data migration
  • Maintenance and development
