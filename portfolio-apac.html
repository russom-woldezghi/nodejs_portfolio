Asia Pacific Rule of Law Conference

As part of the World Justice Project (WJP) Asia regional programmatic work the Asia Pacifice Rule of Law Conference was designed to gather members of the Asia Pacific legal community to convene and work on leading causes of human rights violations in the Asia Pacific.

Using the WordPress platform WJP was able to create an information driven conference website with agendas, speaker and travel information. Using social media and Google Analyitics WJP staff were able to track and respond to site usage and traffic.

Conference participants were able to join private blog dicussions and collobrate on workshops in preperation of the regional conference. Using WordPress's core users account custom WordPress plugins were developed to allow sharing and communications of documents and pages to specific conference participants.

Project Details:

  • User accounts
  • Custom theme
  • Google Analytics and social media
  • Blog for participants and organizers
